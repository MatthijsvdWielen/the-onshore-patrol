What This Is About

As a Dutch surfer, I'm always looking for a surfboard to improve my surfing in the North Sea trenches. What I found is that most of the reviews out there are done by pro surfers in waves that are nothing like what the surfers in the Netherlands are facing. I want to share my honest reviews looking at how a board surfs, how durable it is and what value the board retains over time.

Why Reviews?

Pro-level reviews don't always translate to how a board feels in chunky wind-swell. I want to bridge that gap.
